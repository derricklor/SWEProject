Original Project Description

Your team will design and prototype a new educational video game. This game must be suitable for students between 4th to 6th grade (late elementary school or early middle school). You may choose to narrow the target age range. The target demographic will likely have an effect on the text, concepts, and mechanics that you want to use; Young children may not have the reading, motor, or analytical skills required for some designs.

SRS (SW Requirements Spec)

Using the requirements definition that your team previously created for the client, you will now write a software requirements specification (SRS). Whereas the requirements definition describes the problem from the client’s view, the SRS describes the technical requirements needed by the developers who are working on the project. This specification should be written in terms of the objects or events that can be sensed or detected by your system.
